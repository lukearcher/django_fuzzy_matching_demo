Fuzzy Matching


The purpose of this page is to demonstrate a simple fuzzy matching alogrithm.
To use this please enter the two names to test in the Name 1 and Name 2 boxes.
Then click the Fuzzy Matching button.
This will perform simple fuzzy matching and output 3 separate scores:

  1. Fuzzy Score is the overall score (in order)
  2. Partial Score is done by comparing the shortest name to the others
  3. Token Score is the score without worrying about the order of the names (just in case we are comparing last and first names in the opposite order)

The scores range beteen 0 and 1, with a higher score indicating a higher confidence in the match.
Have fun!
